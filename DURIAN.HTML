About Durian Garden

Durian Garden is a family-run resort built around a working durian orchard. We invite guests to unwind in spacious cottages, taste seasonal fruits, and enjoy meals made from locally sourced ingredients. Our restaurant highlights durian in creative dishes while offering a wide menu for all tastes.
